Groups

Funkamongus

A Blues-based Rock Band with Funk inflections. Plays hard rock, heavy funk, and anything else that makes your nose crinkle.

Performs as a four piece band (drums/vocal, guitar/vocal, bass/vocal & sax/vocal), suited for stages and large venues prepared for spankage.

Based in Kill Devil Hills, NC

Loose Wheel

An improvisation based group with classic roots extending to Bob Dylan, The Talking Heads & many others. An eclectic mix of soul, beachy blues, folk, funk and rock covers, as well as original tunes.

Performs in many different configurations ranging from duo (guitar/flute/violin/vocal & percussion/vocal) to a six piece band (percussion/vocal, keys/vocal, guitar/flute/violin/vocal, guitar/vocal, bass, sax/vocal) and frequents special guests.

Great for anything from dinner sets to outdoor festivals and events.

Based on Hatteras Island, NC

The Notorious Clamslammers

A good timin' Disco Funk band with hints of Pop/Beach Rock.

Performs as a five piece band (keys/vocal, guitar/vocal, bass/vocal, percussion & saxophone).

Suitable for venues both large and small, from festivals to bookstores.

Based in Topsail Beach, NC

Your Mom

A beachy cover band that hits all the radio classics with improvisational twists and turns.

Performs as a three piece (acoustic guitar/vocal, drums/vocal & saxophone) and frequents special guests.

Great entertainment for parties, happy hours, & outdoor events.

Based on Hatteras Island, NC
